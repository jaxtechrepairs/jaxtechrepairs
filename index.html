FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the things people ask us most.

Do you do house calls?
Yes — house calls are my specialty! I come to your home in Crossville so you don't have to pack up your TV or haul in a heavy computer. Just book online and I'll come to you.
What devices do you support?
I support phones (iPhone & Android), tablets (iPad, Samsung Tab, Fire), computers and laptops (Windows & Mac), and smart TVs. Physical repairs like screen and battery replacements are being added as the business grows.
Can you fix my device remotely?
Yes! For most software issues — slow computers, WiFi problems, TV app errors, virus removal — I can connect securely to your device and fix it without anyone leaving home.
How do I book a support appointment?
Click "Book a House Call" on any page. Fill out the quick form — about 2 minutes. Pick your device, describe the issue, choose a time, and I'll confirm shortly. You can also text me directly at 931-248-6663.
Are you available on weekends?
Wednesday is my main day, and I can take appointments most other days except Saturday. I also work full-time as a Wireless Expert at Walmart, so I'll always be upfront about my availability when you book.
What if you can't fix my issue?
Simple — I don't charge you. My No Fix, No Fee policy means you only pay when your problem is resolved. Every completed job is also backed by a 90-day guarantee.

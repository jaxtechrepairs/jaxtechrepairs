Our Story

Why JaxTech Repairs Exists

JaxTech Repairs was founded with a simple goal — to give Crossville residents a reliable, local option for tech support and device help without the stress of driving to a big box store or waiting weeks for a mail-in service.

As someone who works daily as a Wireless Phone Expert at Walmart, helping customers with phones, plans, and device setup is already a big part of what I do. JaxTech Repairs is the natural extension of that — bringing that same helpful, patient approach directly to your home.

Right now the focus is on phones, TVs, tablets, and computers — troubleshooting, setup, performance improvements, and general tech support. Physical repairs like screen replacements and battery swaps are coming as the business continues to grow.

"My goal is simple — honest service, fair pricing, and dependable help from someone local you can trust when technology gives you trouble."

— Owner, JaxTech Repairs
